MY BIO

Jackson Wang (Chinese: 王嘉爾; Korean: 왕잭슨; born Wang Ka-yee on 28 March 1994) is a Hong Kong rapper, singer, songwriter, dancer and fashion designer. He is a member of the South Korean boy band Got7, formed by JYP Entertainment, and the founder of record label Team Wang, where he serves as the creative director and lead designer for its subsidiary fashion brand Team Wang Design.
